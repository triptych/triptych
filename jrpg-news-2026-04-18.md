# JRPG News — April 18, 2026

## Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter — September 17 Worldwide Release Confirmed

Falcom and GungHo Online Entertainment have confirmed that *The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter* will launch worldwide on **September 17, 2026**, across PS5, Switch, Switch 2, and PC via Steam. The announcement, made April 17, also revealed the full English voice cast for the remake.

The release resolves the infamous cliffhanger ending of *Trails in the Sky SC*, the beloved 2006 sequel to the PSP classic. As a pre-order bonus, Japanese PS5, Switch 2, and Switch buyers receive an HD remaster of the original *Trails in the Sky* with high-speed mode and text log improvements.

A premium physical "Ouroboros Box" (Japan exclusive, ¥13,860) includes the game, an arranged music album spanning the Sky saga, sheep-themed cosmetics, a character art book, and a Blu-ray of the 2011–2012 *Trails in the Sky* anime.

## Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave — PEGI 12 Rating Locked In, Still on Track for 2026

*Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave* — the eighteenth mainline Fire Emblem entry and a Nintendo Switch 2 exclusive — has received an official **PEGI 12** rating from European regulators, a strong signal that the game is nearing its release window. Nintendo confirmed in recent financial disclosures that Fortune's Weave remains on track for a 2026 launch and is not at risk of slipping to 2027.

Set around the Heroic Games, a gladiatorial tournament hosted by a divine ruler who promises to grant winners' wishes, the story appears to share continuity with the Fódlan arc (*Three Houses* / *Three Hopes*), with a figure resembling an older Sothis visible in the reveal trailer. No specific release date has been announced beyond the 2026 window.

## Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta — Switch Release Ten Days Away (April 28)

XSEED Games' western release of *Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta* lands on **April 28, 2026**, exclusively on Nintendo Switch in North America and Europe. The game is a rearranged port of *Ys: Memories of Celceta* (originally PS Vita / PS4 / PC), now featuring newly arranged versions of all background music.

Set immediately after the events of *Ys X: Nordics*, the game bridges several gameplay mechanics that would later appear in *Ys VIII* and *Ys IX*, making it an important entry for series newcomers catching up on the timeline.

A physical Day One Edition ($59.99) is available via the Marvelous USA Online Store and select retailers, bundling a 3D acrylic keychain/diorama, a two-disc soundtrack (36 tracks), five art cards, and a 21" × 24" cloth map of Celceta.

## Tales of Series — Older Remasters Promised for 2026, New Mainline Entry in Development

Series producer Yusuke Tomizawa has reiterated that the *Tales of — Remastered Project* will shift its focus to **older, classic entries** in 2026. After the recent wave covering the twelfth (*Graces f*), thirteenth (*Xillia*), and sixteenth (*Berseria*) mainline games, Tomizawa confirmed fans can expect upcoming remaster announcements from earlier in the series' history.

Additionally, a brand-new *Tales of* title remains in active development, though no release window or title has been revealed. The announcements are expected as part of the series' ongoing 30th anniversary celebrations.
