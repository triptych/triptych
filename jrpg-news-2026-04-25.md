# JRPG News — April 25, 2026

## Final Fantasy XIV Coming to Nintendo Switch 2 in August — New Expansion Revealed

Square Enix and producer Naoki Yoshida ("Yoshi-P") dropped a pair of major announcements for *Final Fantasy XIV*:

- **Switch 2 version:** *FFXIV Online* launches on Nintendo Switch 2 in **August 2026**, with full cross-progression across all platforms. A one-month free trial period will be offered at launch. Notably, the Switch 2 version requires a **separate subscription** distinct from the standard PC/PlayStation/Xbox sub — existing subscribers on other platforms receive a 50% discount, and a Nintendo Switch Online subscription is not required.
- **New expansion:** *Final Fantasy XIV: Evercold* was announced, targeting a **January 2027** release.

Both announcements landed on April 24–25 and are generating significant discussion around the subscription structure, which many players are calling an "inconvenient tax" for portable play.

## Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 Hits 8 Million Sales on First Anniversary

To mark its **one-year anniversary** (it launched April 24, 2025), Sandfall Interactive and Kepler Interactive announced that *Clair Obscur: Expedition 33* has sold over **eight million copies** worldwide across PS5, Xbox Series, and PC. The game's trajectory: 500k in 24 hours → 1M in 3 days → 2M in 12 days → 3.3M in 33 days → 8M in one year. To celebrate, a free anniversary update was released on all platforms adding a new haircut style for each playable character alongside minor bug fixes.

## Another Eden Begins Gets September 17 Release Date

*Another Eden Begins* — the premium, standalone console/PC reimagining of the free-to-play mobile JRPG *Another Eden: The Cat Beyond Time and Space* — has been given a firm launch date of **September 17, 2026** for Nintendo Switch 2, Nintendo Switch, and PC via Steam.

Key details:
- Created by director/writer **Masato Kato** (Chrono Trigger, Xenogears, Chrono Cross) with music by **Yasunori Mitsuda**
- A fully self-contained story following Aldo and his sister Feinne — no knowledge of the mobile game required
- Full English voiceovers throughout the main campaign
- Features the "Another Force" mechanic and a New Game Plus mode with ten different endings depending on player choices
- Languages: English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese

## Xenosaga: Pied Piper Coming to Switch and PC via G-MODE Archives+

Announced last week, G-MODE is bringing *Xenosaga: Pied Piper* — originally a 2004 Japanese feature phone RPG — to **Nintendo Switch (Japan eShop)** and **PC (Steam, international)** as a G-MODE Archives+ release. The game is a prequel to the Xenosaga trilogy, following the human life of cyborg Ziggy a century before the events of *Episode I: Der Wille zur Macht*. No release date has been set; the PC Steam version will be available internationally but with Japanese language support only.

## Upcoming This Week: Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta (April 28)

A reminder that *Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta* launches on **Nintendo Switch on April 28** — just three days away. The Western Switch release of the remaster carries a $29.99 digital price with a 15% launch discount through May 4; the physical Day One Edition ($59.99) includes a 2-disc OST, art cards, and a cloth map.
