## LEVEL-5 Vision 2026 Craftsmanship Recap (April 10)

The long-awaited LEVEL-5 showcase aired April 10 at 6:00 AM PT / 9:00 AM ET on the official LEVEL-5 YouTube channel with English and Traditional Chinese support. Key takeaways:

- **Professor Layton and the New World of Steam** — Still coming; received new footage.
- **Snack World: Reloaded** — Newly revealed during the broadcast.
- **Pufflings: Journey Through a Fantasy World** — Another new title shown for the first time.
- **Inazuma Eleven: Victory Road** — Switch 2 physical edition confirmed for Japan on **June 11**, including all updates through the Rising Bond DLC.
- **DECAPOLICE**, **Inazuma Eleven RE**, and **Holy Horror Mansion** — All shown with new gameplay or footage; all carry a non-committal "in development" label with no release windows, continuing the studio's pattern of cautious timelines.

## New Releases This Week

**Fight Life: Vanguard** (StartImpulse / Two Cakes Studio — PC (Steam), PS5, Xbox Series, Switch | $14.99 | April 10)
A tactical turn-based RPG set in a pixel-art dark fantasy world. Players command a mercenary company on the brink of oblivion, with gameplay designed to reward strategic planning and careful positioning. Launched with a 20% introductory discount ($11.99) through April 24.

**Dosa Divas** (Outerloop Games — PC (Steam), Switch, Switch 2, Xbox Series X|S, PS5 | April 14)
A narrative turn-based RPG rooted in South Asian food culture. Sisters Amani and Samara team up with their living spirit-mech Goddess to take down a corporate fast-food empire and restore their villages' cultural heritage. Combat uses timing-based attacks and flavour-matching mechanics. Reviews are mixed — VideoChums calls it "a delicious indie RPG" while others note frustrating recipe systems and underdeveloped world design. Reviewed by RPGFan on April 13.

## Upcoming: Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta — Switch (April 28)

Nihon Falcom's expanded remake of *Ys: Memories of Celceta* (originally PS Vita, 2013) arrives on Nintendo Switch in the Americas on **April 28, 2026**, published by Marvelous. Price: $29.99, with a 15% eShop launch discount available through May 4. Features a rearranged score and Switch-specific optimizations. European release details are expected to align closely.

---

## Announcement: Octopath Traveler TTRPG — English Edition

Square Enix and **Dark Horse** have confirmed the English release of the *Octopath Traveler TRPG Rulebook* for **October 6, 2026**. The 328-page hardcover ($59.99) adapts the tabletop RPG that has been available in Japan since February 2022. It uses 2d6-based rules and incorporates franchise elements such as Jobs, named characters (Therion, Primrose, etc.), and locations from both *Octopath Traveler* and the mobile title *Champions of the Realm*. Players can build custom characters or take control of established heroes.

## Also Worth Noting

**Wardrum** — Highlighted in the Turn Based Lovers April 11 roundup. A turn-based roguelite where players stop the spread of corrupted rhythm magic. Combat is synchronized to the beat of a tribe's wardrum, rewarding well-timed coordination.
