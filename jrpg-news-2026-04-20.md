# JRPG News — April 20, 2026

## Breaking Today

**Wild Arms 4 Returns to PlayStation**
The 2005 PS2 classic from Media.Vision is coming to PS5 and PS4 via emulation, launching April 21. Wild Arms 1–3 are already on the PS Store; this release inches the series closer to a full digital library restoration.

**No Rest for the Wicked PS5 Tease**
Moon Studios told console players "we haven't forgotten you," confirming PS5 interest for their action RPG. The game has now sold 1.7 million copies in Early Access. No timeline given — the signal is tied to an upcoming 1.0 launch window.

**Classic RPGs Revived This Week**
Per RPGFan's weekly roundup (4/19): a 1992 SEGA Genesis RPG is releasing on modern platforms, and a 2004 Japanese feature-phone RPG is receiving its first-ever English localization this week.

## April 2026 Releases

**People of Note** (Released April 7)
Turn-based musical RPG from Iridium Studios / Annapurna Interactive — out now on PC, Xbox Series X|S, PS5, and Switch 2. Worlds and characters are built around distinct musical styles, and the score shifts dynamically during combat.

**Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta** (Late April)
The remake of Ys: Memories of Celceta (PS Vita, 2013) is heading to Nintendo Switch in the final stretch of April.

**Alabaster Dawn** (Early Access May 7 — upcoming)
From Radical Fish Games (CrossCode), this 2.5D action RPG enters Early Access next month.

---

## Upcoming Highlights

**Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter** — Nihon Falcom confirmed a release just one year after its 2025 predecessor, continuing the Trails franchise momentum.

**Trails Beyond the Horizon** — Described as the "beginning of the end" for Falcom's long-running Trails saga; one of 2026's most-anticipated JRPGs.

**Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave** — Intelligent Systems exclusive for Nintendo Switch 2. Players compete in gladiatorial battles called the Heroic Games.

**Granser** — Sandbox JRPG with a living overworld featuring trade systems, airship travel, and dynamic weather in an anime-fantasy world.
