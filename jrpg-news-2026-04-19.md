# JRPG News — April 19, 2026

## G-MODE Archives+ Revives Three Classic Mobile RPGs

During a G-MODE Archives 6th-anniversary stream on April 16, publisher G-MODE announced a trio of classic Japanese mobile RPG ports for Nintendo Switch and PC (Steam), all under the **G-MODE Archives+** label. All releases are Japan-only and Japanese-language only.

### Digital Devil Saga: Avatar Tuner — A's TEST Server (Out Now)

The first of the three is already available as of April 16. *Digital Devil Saga: Avatar Tuner: A's TEST Server* is a complete edition port of the 2006 feature-phone spin-off of Atlus's *Digital Devil Saga* duology. It uses the same Press Turn System found in mainline *Shin Megami Tensei* titles — hitting enemy weaknesses earns extra turns, exploiting them rewards aggressive play. Priced at ¥/\$15.99 on Steam, with a 10% launch discount active through April 30.

### Xenosaga: Pied Piper (April 30)

The most high-profile of the three, *Xenosaga: Pied Piper* is a turn-based RPG originally released for Japanese feature phones on July 14, 2004. The story was written by series creators **Tetsuya Takahashi and Soraya Saga**, and follows the human life of cyborg **Ziggy** — a key supporting character in the Xenosaga trilogy — set a full century before *Xenosaga Episode I: Der Wille zur Macht*. It marks the first time the *Xenosaga* brand has appeared in any official capacity in over 20 years.

The port targets April 30 for Switch eShop and Steam worldwide (Japanese language only).

### Namco Chronicle (TBA 2026)

Announced alongside the above, *Namco Chronicle* is a crossover strategy RPG from 2009 that assembles over 20 characters from classic Namco IPs — including Valkyrie (*The Adventure of Valkyrie*), Gil and Ki (*The Tower of Druaga*), and Momo (*Wonder Momo*). The game is split into three chapters — Fantasy, City, and Space — and blends grid-based tactical battles with adventure story segments, featuring skill animations and combo attacks between party members. No release date was announced.

## Ruinlander — Early Access Now Live (Discount Ends Today)

*Ruinlander*, a solo-developed survival RPG set in a post-nuclear wasteland, launched into Early Access on Steam on **April 12**. The current build focuses on a replayable **Sandbox Mode** where players explore the wastes, scavenge, fight, and trade to survive. A full story campaign and quest chain are planned for later in Early Access.

The introductory 15% launch discount expires **today, April 19**, so players interested in getting in early should act soon. The developer is actively seeking community feedback to shape development priorities.

---

## Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta — Switch Version Coming Late April

Nihon Falcom's *Ys Memoire: The Oath in Felghana* follow-up, *Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta*, is heading to **Nintendo Switch** before the end of April. The game is an enhanced remaster of the 2012 PS Vita title and continues the Ys Memoire series of portable remasters. No exact date beyond "end of April" has been confirmed.
