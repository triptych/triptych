## Big Story: LEVEL-5 Vision 2026 Craftsmanship Showcase

LEVEL-5's annual strategy showcase streamed today at 5:00 AM PDT / 8:00 AM EDT on the official YouTube channel. President Akihiro Hino hosted, and the event delivered several significant updates and new announcements.

### Professor Layton and the New World of Steam — New Trailer + Platform Expansion

A substantial new story trailer dropped today — described by Nintendo Wire as "rather beefy" — offering the best look yet at the seventh mainline Layton entry. Set one year after *Professor Layton and the Unwound Future*, Layton reunites with Luke in a Parisian-inspired town terrorized by a phantom gunman. The trailer teases a kidnapped heiress, a city "driven by relentless progress," and puzzles intertwined with steampunk invention.

Key new details from the trailer:
- **Platforms expanded**: Previously announced for Switch and Switch 2 only — now confirmed for **PlayStation 5** and **PC via Steam** as well.
- **Theme song**: Composed by **Joe Hisaishi**, with lyrics and vocals by **Lilas**.
- **Gameplay features shown**: Coin radar, world map, updated movement, puzzles that cause the town to grow, new companion characters, full 3D scenes, and mouse support (excluding original Switch).
- **Release window**: Still 2026. Hino confirmed the game is "nearing completion" but declined to give a firm date.

### DECAPOLICE — Platforms Revised, Status Update

The long-delayed detective RPG received its first meaningful update in over a year. LEVEL-5 confirmed the game is "steadily progressing," but its platform lineup has changed: DECAPOLICE now targets **Switch 2, PlayStation 5, and PC via Steam**, dropping the original Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4 from the list. The title still carries a 2026 release target, though the showcase trailer listed it as "In Development." A simultaneous anime adaptation remains planned.

### Snack World: Reloaded — Announced

A full-blown remake of *The Snack World: Trejarers* was announced for **Switch 2, PlayStation 5, and PC via Steam**. The remake features overhauled visuals and mechanics, plus a new story mode where players take on the role of **Chup**, the protagonist of the *Snack World* anime series — a new angle not present in the original game. No release window was given.

### Pufflings: Journey Through a Fantasy World — Revealed

LEVEL-5 and mobile developer **NHN PlayArt** jointly announced *Pufflings: Journey Through a Fantasy World* for **iOS and Android**. Described as a "fusion puzzle" game, further details are sparse but the title represents LEVEL-5's continued mobile push alongside their console work.

### Additional Updates

- **Inazuma Eleven RE** (remake of the original *Inazuma Eleven*) and **Holy Horror Mansion** (next in the Yokai Watch lineage) were shown in a sizzle reel. No new release dates for either.

## Today's Launch

**Fight Life: Vanguard** (StartImpulse / Two Cakes Studio — PC via Steam | April 10; PS, Xbox, Switch versions TBA)

A retro-styled tactical turn-based RPG set in a dark fantasy world where ancient gods have faded and mortal ambition breeds chaos. Players lead a squad of mercenaries through handcrafted scenarios, recruiting warriors who each follow distinct growth paths. Combat rewards tight positional planning in close-quarters encounters. The game supports 13 languages including Japanese and Korean. Console versions are planned but unscheduled.
