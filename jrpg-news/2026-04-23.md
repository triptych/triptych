# JRPG News — April 23, 2026

## Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter Gets September Release Date

Falcom confirmed that **Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter** launches **September 17, 2026** worldwide. The announcement (April 17) also revealed a new trailer and the full voice cast. The game releases simultaneously on PS5, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, and PC via Steam. It picks up one day after the events of the first chapter.

Sources: [Gematsu](https://www.gematsu.com/2026/04/trails-in-the-sky-2nd-chapter-launches-september-17) · [NoisyPixel](https://noisypixel.net/trails-in-the-sky-2nd-chapter-worldwide-release-september-17-2026/) · [RPG Site](https://www.rpgsite.net/news/19218-falcom-reveals-trails-in-sky-2nd-chapter-set-to-release-in-fall-2026-worldwide-for-playstation-5-switch-2-switch-pc)

## Final Fantasy XIV Patch 7.5 "Trail to the Heavens" Dropping April 28

Square Enix revealed the full trailer and details for **FFXIV Patch 7.5: Trail to the Heavens** on April 17. The patch arrives April 28 and introduces the **Beastmaster limited job** while concluding the Echoes of Vana'diel alliance raid series. The **FFXIV Fan Festival 2026** runs April 24–26, with free Twitch chat badges for viewers.

Sources: [Massively OP](https://massivelyop.com/2026/04/17/final-fantasy-xiv-reveals-its-trailer-and-more-details-for-patch-7-5-trail-to-the-heavens/) · [AllKeyShop](https://www.allkeyshop.com/blog/en-us/final-fantasy-14-patch-7-5-trail-to-the-heavens-release-news-d/)
