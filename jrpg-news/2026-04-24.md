# JRPG News — April 24, 2026

## FFXIV Fan Festival 2026 Kicks Off in Anaheim — 8.0 Reveal Expected

The **FINAL FANTASY XIV Fan Festival 2026** opened today at the Anaheim Convention Center (running April 24–25). The headline event is Naoki Yoshida's keynote (1:00–3:15 PM ET), where he is widely expected to unveil the **FFXIV 8.0 expansion** name and broad story direction. Yoshida teased the reveal in advance, saying attendees should expect "some information that might make people think, 'You're revealing this even though patch 7.5 hasn't been released yet?'" He also promised "even bigger storylines and systems" beyond the typical Fan Festival scope. All stage events are streamed free worldwide. The next expansion is currently projected for **late 2026 or early 2027**. Square Enix is also making chapters of **FFXIV: Eorzea Academy** and **Saki & FFXIV** manga available free via Manga Up during the event.

## Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta Launches April 28 on Switch

Falcom and NIS America confirmed that **Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta** arrives on **Nintendo Switch in the Americas on April 28**. The remake of the 2013 PS Vita classic features a rearranged soundtrack and is priced at **$29.99** digitally (15% launch discount through May 4). A physical **Day One Edition ($59.99)** includes a 3D acrylic keychain/diorama set, a two-disc OST (36 tracks), five art cards, and a 21"×14" cloth map of Celceta.

Sources: [Final Weapon](https://finalweapon.net/2026/04/21/ys-memoire-revelations-in-celceta-review-nintendo-switch/) · [GoNintendo](https://gonintendo.com/contents/58576-ys-memoire-revelations-in-celceta-comes-to-switch-april-28th-2026) · [Nintendo Everything](https://nintendoeverything.com/ys-memoire-revelations-in-celceta-confirms-nintendo-switch-release-date-new-trailer/)

## Kyoto Xanadu Details New Characters and Battle System

Falcom released fresh details on **Kyoto Xanadu**, the next entry in its Xanadu series. New character profiles and combat mechanics were shared, spotlighting the **"Dual Dimensional" battle system** — players switch between 2D sidescroller traversal and full 3D environments on the fly using special equipment called "Soul Devices." Set in an alternate-history Kyoto where the capital never moved, players explore a labyrinth called Xanadu while building bonds between dungeon runs. The game launches **July 16 in Japan and Asia** on PS5, Switch 2, Switch, and PC via Steam; a Western release partner is still being coordinated.

Sources: [Gematsu](https://www.gematsu.com/2026/04/kyoto-xanadu-details-more-characters-battle-system) · [GoNintendo](https://gonintendo.com/contents/59528-kyoto-xanadu-s-characters-battle-system-and-more-detailed) · [RPGamer](https://rpgamer.com/2026/04/kyoto-xanadu-introduces-more-characters-combat-details/)

## Wild Arms 4 Now Available on PS4 and PS5

The classic PS2-era JRPG **Wild Arms 4** is now playable on modern hardware, having been added to the **PlayStation Plus Premium Game Catalog** on April 21. The game joins the growing library of classic PlayStation titles accessible via the service.

## Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave — 2026 Release Window

Nintendo's next mainline tactical RPG, **Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave**, remains on track for a **2026 release** though no specific date has been set. Players will take part in gladiatorial battles known as the Heroic Games, ruled by a Divine Sovereign. The game is among the most anticipated Nintendo Switch 2 titles of the year.
