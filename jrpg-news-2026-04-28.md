# JRPG News — April 28, 2026

## Today's Launch: Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta (Switch)

*Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta* officially released today on Nintendo Switch in North America and Europe at $29.99, with a 15% launch discount through May 4. Reviews that had been held until today are now live, and the consensus lands in mixed-to-positive territory.

- **Nintendo Life** called it "a simple yet fun adventure," praising the combat and characters for carrying the experience even where the plot falls flat.
- **CGMagazine** gave it a generally favorable write-up, highlighting the newly recorded and re-interpreted soundtrack from Falcom Sound Team jdk as a standout addition.
- **MonsterVine** was more critical, subtitling their review "Memories of Mediocrity" and noting it offers little beyond what was already available on PC.
- **Nintendo World Report** and **oprainfall** both landed somewhere in the middle — fans of Falcom's action RPGs will find it comfortable and enjoyable; newcomers may not be wowed.
- A recurring complaint across outlets: the absence of a **turbo mode**, which was present in last year's *Ys: The Oath in Felghana* remaster.
- On **Switch 2 with handheld boost mode**, reviewers noted a noticeably improved visual experience that still holds 60fps in portable play.
- **RPG Site** published a comparison review against the PS5 and PC versions, finding no substantial differences beyond the $5 price premium on Switch.

## Tomorrow's Launch: NTE: Neverness to Everness (PS5 / PC / Mobile)

The open-world action RPG *NTE: Neverness to Everness*, developed by Hotta Studio (Tower of Fantasy) and published by Perfect World Games, launches globally **April 29** on PS5, PC, and mobile, with all servers going live simultaneously at 3:00 AM UTC.

Notable pre-launch details:
- A **Persona 5 music collaboration** is confirmed — players can hear tracks including "Beneath the Mask" and "Wake Up Your Hero" via their in-game avatar's walkman or car radio. Persona characters do not appear; it's a music-only crossover.
- A **Porsche 918 Spyder** collaboration is also planned, arriving in a post-launch update rather than at day one.
- The PlayStation Blog confirmed the game is free-to-play with a detailed breakdown of launch content.

## Beast of Reincarnation (Game Freak) — Pre-Orders Open, August 4 Release

Pre-orders are now live for *Beast of Reincarnation*, the ambitious action RPG from **Game Freak** (best known as the developer of mainline Pokémon). A new trailer released alongside the pre-order announcement on April 25 showed extended combat sequences and previously unseen cutscenes.

- **Platforms:** PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S
- **Release date:** August 4, 2026
- **Standard edition:** $60 | **Deluxe edition:** $70
- **Pre-order bonuses (standard):** 30,000 Amber in-game currency + Brown Shiba Skin for the companion character Koo
- **Deluxe extras:** Big Dipper weapon, Oni's Hat cosmetic, Black Shiba Skin, 100,000 Amber

The game features a character named Koo and a currency/crafting system built around Amber. It marks a significant departure for Game Freak from their Pokémon work.

Sources: [RPGamer](https://rpgamer.com/2026/04/beast-of-reincarnation-starts-pre-orders-with-new-trailer/) | [Gematsu](https://www.gematsu.com/2026/04/beast-of-reincarnation-pre-orders-now-available) | [RPGFan](https://www.rpgfan.com/2026/04/25/beast-of-reincarnation-pre-orders/)

## Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter — September 17, 2026 Release Date Confirmed

Nihon Falcom confirmed a **September 17, 2026** global release for *Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter*, the remake of the beloved *The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky SC* (originally on PSP). A new trailer and full voice cast were revealed alongside the date.

- **Platforms:** Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 5, PC (Steam)
- Physical editions are available for all console versions
- The story follows Senior Bracer Estelle Bright searching for her adoptive brother Joshua across the kingdom of Liberl, continuing directly from the first chapter remake
- New additions include new Crafts, S-Crafts, and a **Brave Rush** mechanic layered into the hybrid real-time/turn-based combat

Sources: [Push Square](https://www.pushsquare.com/news/2026/04/trails-in-the-sky-2nd-chapter-puts-the-remade-jrpg-classic-on-ps5-this-september) | [RPGamer](https://rpgamer.com/2026/04/trails-in-the-sky-2nd-chapter-launching-in-september/)

## Wild Arms 4 Added to PlayStation Plus Premium

Sony quietly added *Wild Arms 4* (2005, PS2) to the **PlayStation Plus Premium** catalog this week, available at no additional cost for Premium subscribers on PS4 and PS5. The game benefits from Sony's PSSR (PlayStation Spectral Super Resolution) emulation layer, which brings modern controller support and performance stability improvements to the 20-year-old title.

Wild Arms 4 is notable for its hexagonal grid-based battle system, a departure from both its predecessors and from conventional JRPG combat — making it an interesting pick for players who never experienced it at launch.

Sources: [Push Square](https://www.pushsquare.com/news/2026/04/20-year-old-sony-jrpg-makes-surprise-return-to-ps5-ps4-this-week-free-on-ps-plus-premium) | [CBR](https://www.cbr.com/wild-arms-4-playstation-return/)

## Recent Release: AVILION forever (Switch / iOS / Android)

*AVILION forever*, a turn-based RPG originally released as a Japan-only feature phone title, launched on **Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android** on April 23 with an English localization. It's priced at **$7.99 on Switch** (with a 10% launch discount) and **$5.99 on mobile**. The game casts players as heroes trained at the Avilion Academy to fight back against demon forces. It's a compact, classic-feeling JRPG aimed at fans of the genre's earlier era.

Source: [RPGamer New Release Round-Up (April 23)](https://rpgamer.com/2026/04/new-release-round-up-april-23-2026/)
