## Today's Launches

**People of Note** (Iridium Studios / Annapurna Interactive — PC, PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2)
Out today at $24.99 (10% launch discount). A musical turn-based RPG following Cadence, a pop singer locked out of the prestigious Noteworthy Song Contest, as she recruits musicians from genre-specific cities across the world of Note. Battles are structured around musical stanzas with genre-mashup attacks. Campaign runs 15–25 hours.

**Final Fantasy IV Pixel Remaster — Xbox Game Pass** (now live)
Previously announced; joins Game Pass today on Cloud, Xbox Series X|S, and PC — continuing the monthly FF Pixel Remaster rollout (FF1 January, FF2 February, FF3 March).

## New Releases (Non-JRPG Adjacent)

**Starfield — PlayStation 5 Launch + Free Lanes Update + Terran Armada DLC** (Bethesda / Xbox Game Studios)
Bethesda's space RPG arrives on PS5 today ($49.99 / $69.99 premium), ending its Xbox/PC exclusivity. The massive free "Free Lanes" update and the new Terran Armada story DLC launch simultaneously across all platforms. PS5-specific features include DualSense adaptive trigger support, light bar health/ship tracking, and touchpad shortcuts. PS5 Pro players get Visual (4K/30fps) and Performance (60fps) modes.

## Rumors & Speculation

**Persona 1 Origins / Persona 2 Duality — Merchandise Discovered on Amazon**
Official Atlus-branded apparel and phone cases using the previously unknown names "Persona 1 Origins" and "Persona 2 Duality" have appeared on Amazon. Atlus has announced no games under these titles, but merchandise reveals have historically preceded product announcements. With the Persona series 30th anniversary underway and producer Kazuhisa Wada on record saying he'd like to remake the originals "someday," fan speculation is high that remasters or remakes of the original PS1 Persona titles are in development. No confirmation.

**Xenoblade Chronicles — Voice Actress Listing Points to New Project**
Caitlin Thorburn (KOS-MOS in *Xenoblade Chronicles 2*) updated her professional casting profile to include a new Xenoblade Chronicles project for 2026. Analysts believe the most likely outcome is a *Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Definitive Edition* for Switch 2, consistent with how Nintendo handled XC1. A new *Xenoblade Chronicles 4* remains possible given it has been four years since XC3. Nintendo and Monolith Soft have not commented.

## Game Details & Updates

**Kyoto Xanadu** (Nihon Falcom — PS5, Switch, Switch 2, PC | Japan: July 16, 2026)
Falcom released new character and battle system details. New characters: Lear Beresford (13-year-old magical-tools prodigy and party leader), Eliza Alcott (18-year-old rapier-wielding disciplinary officer), Hosen Suruga (18-year-old leader of the Abyssal Investigators), and Luana Calderone (18-year-old "Saint of Siena"). The "Dual Dimensional" battle system lets players switch between 2D and 3D action via special "Soul Devices" equipment. A new April 2026 calendar featuring protagonist Rei was also released.

**Utawarerumono: Past and Present Rediscovered** (AQUAPLUS — PS5, Switch 2 Japan May 28 / PC worldwide)
AQUAPLUS detailed new characters and field/battle systems. New characters: Shunrai (matriarch and adoptive mother of Mikazuchi), Raiko ("Raiko the Sage," a coolheaded Pillar General strategist), and sisters Ashta and Nava (Arva Shulan operatives on a secret mission in Yamato). Field system additions include back-attack combat bonuses, rideable Woptors for faster traversal, warp-from-anywhere fast travel, and a new Area Conquest feature for claiming map segments by defeating area rulers.

**Digimon Story: Time Stranger — DLC Pack 3 and 2027 Expansion Announced** (Bandai Namco)
Announced at Digimon Con 2026: DLC Episode Pack 3 arrives between April–June 2026, with a "large-scale DLC expansion" following in 2027. The game also comes to Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 on July 10, 2026 — broadening availability beyond its current PS5/Xbox Series/PC release.

## Industry News

**Atlus Raises Salaries 15%, Reduces Fixed Overtime** (effective April 2026)
Atlus implemented compensation improvements this month: new graduate monthly salaries rise from ¥300,000 to ¥330,000 (~$2,070 USD), base annual salaries increase ~15% across full-time and contract staff, and fixed overtime drops from 30 to 20 hours/month. Atlus joins Capcom and Konami in raising wages amid broader industry layoffs, with the Persona 30th anniversary development cycle as added incentive to retain talent.

## Books & Tabletop

**Octopath Traveler TRPG Rulebook — English Release October 6, 2026** (Dark Horse / Square Enix)
The *Octopath Traveler* TRPG Rulebook, previously Japanese-only, gets its first English edition on October 6, 2026 as a 328-page hardcover ($59.99) published by Dark Horse. The 2d6-based rules system lets players explore Orsterra with original characters or pre-built versions of the game's heroes.
