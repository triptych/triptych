## Dissidia Duellum Final Fantasy — Firion and Balthier Arrive Today

Two iconic Final Fantasy characters join the roster of Square Enix's 3vs3 arena action title **Dissidia Duellum Final Fantasy** today, April 14.

**Firion** (Final Fantasy II) is a Melee-type fighter who wields a wide variety of weapons on the front line. He plays as a dedicated short-range damage dealer in the same vein as Cloud or Lightning, and is voiced by Hikaru Midorikawa, reprising his classic role.

**Balthier** (Final Fantasy XII) is a Ranged-type fighter who attacks hard and fast, with a focus on defeating rivals at a distance. His voice actor Hiroaki Hirata also returns from FFXII.

To mark their arrival, a **Sealed Crystals (Firion / Balthier) event** is running in Casual and Ranked Battles, giving players a chance to unlock Ability items for both new characters. Character preview trailers for each were published Sunday, April 12.

## Persona 4 Revival — Pre-Orders Now Open Across All Platforms

Atlus's upcoming **Persona 4 Revival** — a full-scale remake of the celebrated 2008 PS2 JRPG — is now available to wishlist and pre-order on the **PlayStation Store**, **Xbox Store**, and **Steam**. The listing has surfaced two packages on SteamDB, suggesting separate Standard and Deluxe editions, and metadata reportedly confirms inclusion of **Marie / Golden content**, previously exclusive to Persona 4 Golden.

The game is confirmed for **PlayStation 5, Windows, and Xbox Series X/S** and remains on track for a release within Sega Sammy's fiscal year 2026–2027 window (April 2026 – March 2027). No specific release date has been announced.

This adds a concrete pre-order step to what was previously just a listing — yesterday's coverage of the Persona 30th Anniversary concert noted Sega's investor filings confirmed P4 Revival within the current fiscal year.

## MONGIL: STAR DIVE — Free-to-Play Monster-Taming Action RPG Launches Tomorrow

Netmarble's **MONGIL: STAR DIVE** launches globally on **April 15** for **PC (via its own launcher), iOS, and Android** as a free-to-play title. A console release (PlayStation 5 and Xbox) is confirmed but will come at a later, unannounced date.

Built on **Unreal Engine 5**, the game takes place in a world called **Belana** and combines real-time tag-team combat with a monster-collection system centered on creatures called **Monsterlings**. Unlike traditional monster-taming games, Monsterlings are synthesised as well as collected, adding a crafting dimension to the team-building loop.

The game is a spiritual successor to Netmarble's 2013 mobile title **Monster Taming** and has been in wide pre-registration across the Epic Games Store, Google Play, and the App Store in the lead-up to launch.

Tickets are $249.99 ($264.99 after fees). The Fan Festival tour continues with dates in Europe and Japan later in 2026.

## Persona 6 Rumor: Xbox Games Showcase June 7 Named as Reveal Window

A leaker claim circulating since late March has gained renewed attention this week: a source alleges that **Persona 6** will make its world debut at the **Xbox Games Showcase** expected on **June 7, 2026**. Push Square and My Nintendo News covered the rumor in March; no corroboration from Atlus has followed.

Context: Producer Kazuhisa Wada confirmed last year that 2026 would bring "opportunities to talk about future developments for the series," and the official Persona 30th Anniversary website already references "the next chapter for the series." The franchise's 30th anniversary falls on **September 30, 2026**, making summer showcases a plausible window for a reveal ahead of that milestone.

The Persona 6 rumor remains unconfirmed. Take with appropriate skepticism.
