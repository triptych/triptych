# JRPG News — April 29, 2026

## NTE: Neverness to Everness — Now Live

*NTE: Neverness to Everness* officially launched today on **PC, PlayStation 5, iOS, Android, and Mac**, with all platforms going live simultaneously at 3:00 AM UTC. Early launch day impressions are notably positive.

Critics and players are highlighting the game's ambition: it attempts to be a gacha RPG, a city life simulator, a driving game, an anomaly-hunting narrative experience, and a management sandbox all at once — and the general consensus is that it largely succeeds. Hethereau, the game's urban open world, is being called a "genuinely lived-in space" rather than a backdrop, with the tension between mundane city life and anomaly sequences giving it a distinct visual identity.

- **Combat** is approachable on the surface but gains depth through team synergy and coordinated ultimates
- **Exploration and city activities** carry equal weight to fighting in the game's loop
- **Gacha generosity** is drawing attention: multiple free characters and generous free pulls are positioning NTE as one of the more player-friendly gacha launches in recent memory
- The Persona 5 music collaboration (in-game walkman/car radio with tracks including "Beneath the Mask") and a post-launch Porsche 918 Spyder collaboration were previously confirmed

## Final Fantasy VII Rebirth Demo Drops on Switch 2 and Xbox Series X|S

Square Enix quietly released a **free demo** for *Final Fantasy VII Rebirth* yesterday (April 28) on **Nintendo Switch 2** and **Xbox Series X|S**, ahead of the game's full release on those platforms on **June 3, 2026**.

Key details:
- The demo covers **Chapters 1 and 2** and includes the Streamlined Progression system
- **Save data transfers** to the full game — no progress is lost
- Playing the demo unlocks bonus items for the full game: a **Kupo Charm** (increases chance of collecting extra materials) and a **Survival Set**
- The **physical Switch 2 edition** is priced at **$49.99** and includes an exclusive **Magic: The Gathering "Zack Fair" promo card** featuring variant art by Tetsuya Nomura, plus a reversible inlay with alternate cover art

The demo is available now on the Nintendo eShop and Microsoft Store.

## Final Fantasy XIV Patch 7.5 — Trail to the Heavens Is Live

*Final Fantasy XIV* Patch 7.5, subtitled **Trail to the Heavens**, launched yesterday (April 28) and opens the first of a two-part conclusion to the Dawntrail patch cycle story.

Content added in 7.5:
- **New main scenario quests** continuing the Dawntrail storyline
- **The Clyteum** — a new dungeon
- **Enuo** trial in Normal and Extreme difficulties
- **Shinryu's Domain (Unreal)** — the latest Unreal trial
- **Echoes of Vana'diel** alliance raid — finale chapter
- A new **PvP Crystalline Conflict arena**
- **Duty Support** additions: The Dusk Vigil and Shisui of the Violet Tides
- **Free Trial update:** the Free Trial now includes the critically acclaimed **Shadowbringers** expansion, previously gated behind the full game purchase

## Final Fantasy XIV: Evercold — Full Expansion Details

While the *Evercold* expansion was announced at Fan Fest last Friday, the full feature breakdown deserves a deeper look now that the dust has settled.

**Evercold** is the sixth expansion for *Final Fantasy XIV*, targeting a **January 2027** release across PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch 2, Windows, Mac, and Steam.

Key features revealed at Fan Fest 2026 in Anaheim (April 24–25):
- **Two new jobs**, both original to FFXIV rather than returning jobs from earlier Final Fantasy titles: one **tank** and one **physical ranged DPS**
- **Level cap increase**: 100 → 110
- **New Job Action system** that introduces two play modes for every job:
  - *Reborn Mode* — preserves current action sets and rotation structure for existing players
  - *Evolved Mode* — offers different actions and rotations with fewer required inputs and a sharper focus on job identity, aimed at accessibility
- **Evangelion alliance raid series** — a 24-man collaboration with the anime franchise, developed in partnership with khara, Inc.
- The expansion kicks off the **Godless Realms Saga** story arc, continuing the Warrior of Light's journey after Dawntrail
