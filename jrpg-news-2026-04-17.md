# JRPG News — April 17, 2026

## Persona 5: The Phantom X — Version 3.6 Update (Goro Akechi)

Version 3.6 of the free-to-play *Persona 5: The Phantom X* went live on April 16, bringing Goro "Crow" Akechi from *Persona 5 Royal* as a new 5-Star playable unit. Akechi is a Sweeper-style character with Robin Hood as his Persona, built around area-of-effect attacks that hit multiple enemies at once.

The update includes the "Crossroads of Fate: Laboratory Arc Part 2" crossover story event, where players explore a new Palace featuring fresh boss battles and explore a dramatic moment between Joker, Kasumi, and Akechi from P5R. The event runs through April 30. Akechi's banner remains active through May 21.

A new Synergy Story for Manaka Nagao is also included, and Akechi can be found at Café Leblanc in Yongen-Jaya for City Life activities and rewards.

## Granblue Fantasy: Relink — Endless Ragnarok Confirmed for July 9

Cygames' expansion *Granblue Fantasy: Relink – Endless Ragnarok* has a locked-in release date of **July 9, 2026** for Switch 2, PS5, PS4, and Steam. Originally announced in February's Nintendo Direct Partner Showcase, this is the first time the game comes to Nintendo hardware.

Key additions over the base game:
- **New playable characters:** Beatrix (sword-based close-range) and Eustace (gun-based long-range)
- **New solo mode:** the Conflux, offering fresh endgame challenges
- **New co-op quest tiers** and additional bosses
- **Master traits** to push characters beyond existing limits
- **Crossplay** across all platforms; local wireless multiplayer on Switch 2

Pricing: $59.99 standard / $79.99 special edition. Existing *Relink* owners can upgrade for $29.99.

## Upcoming Indie Release: Odyssey of Dremid'ir (April 24)

*Odyssey of Dremid'ir*, a hand-drawn 2D JRPG by solo developer Chiaroscuro Entertainment, releases on **April 24, 2026** on Steam — one week away. The game follows Codrin, an unwitting mage navigating the post-apocalyptic realm of Dremid'ir, exploring ruined cities, deep caverns, and alien worlds while mastering volatile magic.

The project has been in development since November 2021 and draws explicit inspiration from *Chrono Trigger* and *Final Fantasy VI*. Physical copies will debut the same weekend at the **Midwest Gaming Classic** convention in Milwaukee, WI, in a limited run.
