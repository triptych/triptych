# JRPG News — April 16, 2026

## Recent Releases

- **People of Note** (April 7) — Out now on Windows, PlayStation, and Switch 2. Music is front and center: worlds, characters, and combat are all built around different musical styles, with the score shifting dynamically during gameplay.
- **Fight Life: Vanguard** (April 10) — Released earlier this month across platforms.

## Coming Later in April

- **Ys Memoire: Revelations in Celceta** — Arriving on Switch at the end of April. A remake of the 2013 PS Vita title from Falcom.

## Upcoming in 2026

- **Alabaster Dawn** — Entering Early Access on May 7.
- **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales** — Launches June 18 on consoles and PC.
- **Kyoto Xanadu** — Confirmed July 16 release date for Japan and Asia.
- **Trails in the Sky 2nd Chapter** — Sequel to Trails in the Sky 1st Chapter, picking up one day after the first game ends. Date TBD.
- **Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave** — Nintendo's headline JRPG for 2026. Date TBD.
- **Another Eden Begins** — Confirmed for September 16.

## PS Plus

- **Wild Arms 4** is joining PS Plus this month, making the PS2 classic available on modern platforms.
